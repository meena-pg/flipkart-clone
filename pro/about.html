FLIPKART STORY

From a small bookstore to India’s e-commerce giant

Back in 2007, we started Flipkart with a mission to make books accessible online. With limited resources but big dreams, we worked out of a small apartment and focused on delivering a reliable shopping experience.

Today, Flipkart powers shopping for millions of Indians—offering everything from electronics to fashion—making online shopping easy, affordable, and trusted.
